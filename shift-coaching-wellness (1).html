Specialized Modalities

IFS, sandtray, sound-based nervous system protocols

IFS Sessions
Internal Family Systems helps you understand and heal different parts of yourself with compassion. Strengthens your connection to your core Self — the calm, grounded inner leader.
TraumaAnxietyAttachment
Sandtray & Sandplay Therapy
Expressive, experiential approaches using sand and miniature figures to support healing — especially helpful for trauma, attachment wounds, and clients who struggle to verbalize experiences.
ExpressiveEmbodied
Safe and Sound Protocol (SSP)
An evidence-based listening intervention grounded in Polyvagal Theory. Uses specially filtered music to regulate the vagus nerve, improve stress resilience, and support emotional connection.
Nervous SystemSensory
Rest and Restore Protocol (RRP)
Clinical-grade listening therapy using scientifically engineered acoustics to promote deep relaxation and optimal recovery for chronic stress, trauma, and functional disorders.
RecoveryRegulation
